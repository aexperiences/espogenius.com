What ESPO Genius is

Expert-only information, finally said plainly.

Every Genius app takes something noisy and gatekept and hands you the point — in a sentence you'd actually say out loud.

01

Plain English, always

No charts to decode, no jargon to look up. Just what's happening and why, in words a beginner gets on the first read.

02

It shows its work

You don't get a verdict from a black box. You see the reasoning — the case for, the case against, and how sure it actually is.

The backbone

One honest engine under every ESPO Genius app.

Specialists each read one lens. A pacemaker reconciles them and writes the call in plain English — with a confidence score it has to earn, not one it hands out. In MarketNarc, the three are named:

Pat
the fundamentals lens

Earnings, margins, input costs, valuation. Reads the business underneath the ticker — and is free to land bullish or bearish.

Rex
the technicals lens

Price, volume, trend, where it sits in its range. Reads what the chart is doing — without making you read one.

Bart
the pacemaker · the voice

Reconciles Pat and Rex, scores how sure to be, and writes the plain-English call. Decides and explains — never a silent black box.

It's the same idea behind ESPO Drama's story engine — two voices argue, a third won't ship until it clears the bar. Honesty built into the architecture, not bolted on.
