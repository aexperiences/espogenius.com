Plain-English summary — the full legal policy will be posted before MarketNarc opens to the public.

What we collect

Right now, ESPO Genius is in production and there's no app to sign into. If you email us to join the early-access list, we keep your email address so we can tell you when MarketNarc opens — nothing more.

When MarketNarc launches, an account will need basic details (like your email), payments will be handled by Stripe (we never see or store your full card number), and connecting a brokerage for paper or live trading will go through Alpaca under their security and terms.

What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't hand it to advertisers. We ask for the least we need to run the product and tell you what's happening and why.

Not financial advice

MarketNarc is an educational tool. Its calls are explanations, not recommendations to buy or sell, and nothing here is personalized investment advice. You always make the decision.

Reaching a real person

Questions about your information? Email anthonye@aexperiences.studio and a real person will answer.
